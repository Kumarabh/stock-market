TOP STOCK BROKERS

A stock broker plays an essential role in the buying and selling of securities in India. Since stockbrokers play an important role, it is crucial to choose the most suitable stockbroker for yourself. They help you in opening a Demat and Trading Account for your trading and investment needs. A Demat Account is the one that holds your securities in a dematerialized format. It is like bank accounts expect the fact that it holds securities and not cash. A Trading Account, on the other hand, is required for carrying out buying and selling of securities in the stock market Before discussing about the top stockbroker in India, let us begin with understanding who a stockbroker is?

Who is a stockbroker?

A stockbroker is either an individual or an organization that possesses the licenses to participate in the securities market on behalf of its clients. He mainly plays the role of an agent and acts as a link between Demat Account holders and depositories. Since a stockbroker acts as an agent for buying and selling of securities, he charges a commission amount known as a brokerage for rendering his services. He performs a variety of services for his investors. Every time he buys and sells securities on behalf of his clients, he makes sure that the best price is obtained for the investor i.e., for his client.

Types of stock brokers?
Full-service Broker:

A full-service broker offers a large variety of services to its client rather than just offering the facility of buying and selling of shares. These services include research and advice, tax-saving tips, retirement planning, and much more. They provide expertise to investors who are willing to put their money in the market but lack the substantial time required for the same. In certain cases, full-service brokers also provide customized services to its clients regarding portfolio management, financial planning, and wealth management services. It becomes extremely convenient to invest when dealing in the stock market through a full-service broker.

Discount Brokers:

A discount broker, unlike a full-service broker, provides its services at a very low rate of brokerage charges. Most of the discount brokers are technology driven brokers, and hence their operational cost is very low. They only provide a trading platform to their client for buying and selling of shares. These are no additional services provided as the charges charged by these brokers are very low. Discount brokers are suitable for investors who are capable of investing and trading on their own and do not require expert hand-holding in the context of stock market trading and investments. The online trading software provided by them is usually free of cost.

Top stockbrokers in India

When talking about the Indian stock market, there are a number of stockbrokers in the market who are willing to extend their services to their clients. In such a scenario, it becomes difficult to choose the best stockbroker for oneself.

Check the background of your stockbroker. Before choosing and trusting a stockbroker for you, make it a point to run a strict check for their background. Look for client reviews, their past performance, their experience in the industry, and any other relevant information before trusting them.

Check the minimum amount of balance you are required to keep with the brokerage firm you are scrutinizing. The amount of minimum balance varies from broker to broker.

Look for the brokerage charged by different stockbrokers for their services. Compare the brokerage charges charged by different brokers and then choose wisely. A low brokerage will help you save more amount of money.

Look for the additional services offered by your broker. Check if they are providing any other service apart from buying and selling of shares.

Check for the customer support services offered by your broker. A good customer support plays an important role when choosing a stockbroker.
